Lord of Beasts

Destiny Playbook

You more closely relate to the wild animals of the hunt than you do to your own kin. The beasts respect you and know you as one of their own.

Requirements

To become the Lord of Beasts, you must have Bonds with at least three different Beasts, and three Bonds with at least one Beast.

The Lord of Beasts starts with Queen of the Wild.
