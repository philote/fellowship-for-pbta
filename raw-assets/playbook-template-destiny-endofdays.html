end of days

Destiny Playbook

They say the Harbinger heralds the coming of evil and and end to all goodly things. But one has to wonder if the Harbinger themselves isn't partially at fault for that.

Requirements

To become the End of Days, you must earn the Fellowship of the Harbingers. This Destiny is only allowed in a game with the Overlord.

The End of Days is a Threat to the World, and starts with Are We Not The Same, You And I?
