Ascended

Destiny Playbook

You have become more than you once were, more than anyone should ever be.

You have gained truly unparalleled power, but at what cost?

Requirements

To become the Ascended, you must take a Source of Power for yourself. This Destiny is only allowed in a game with the Overlord.

The Ascended starts with either Inconceivable Truth or Infinite Power, your choice. You have also become a Threat to the World, and the Overlord always knows where you are.
