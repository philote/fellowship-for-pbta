custom moves

invincible soul

When you would get Taken Out, heal 2 damage instead. This does not stop the Overlord from making you An Offer You Can't Refuse.

When the Overlord makes you An Offer You Can't Refuse, you will always accept their offer, but your power is great enough that you can negotiate. Add a caveat of your choosing to their terms.

unparalleled skill

When you take damage in Melee combat, you may immediately deal damage back to them. Ranged attacks cannot hit you - you will always dodge or deflect them, if it is remotely possible for you to do so.

This Move doesn't work against a Threat to the World.

unstoppable force

You never take the 6- result to Overcome, Keep Them Busy, or Get Away. When you roll a 6- on those Moves, act as though you rolled a 7.

You can no longer compromise, retreat, or surrender, for any reason. When you take a course of action, you become completely committed to it.

untouchable beauty

Nothing can leave its mark upon you, not anymore. You do not suffer the ravages of time, your wounds never scar, your form cannot be changed, and you cannot be touched or held by another (although you may hold or touch them).

You no longer relate to those around you, and your Bonds weaken. You can never have more than one Bond with anyone. Erase your extra Bonds now.

custom moves

invincible general

You never take damage from ordinary physical attacks. A Threat to the World can still physically harm you, and high powered explosives or artillery can also harm you, but anything short of that is useless.


off with their head

When you could Finish Them with a Melee weapon, you may kill them instead of rolling.

If they are the Overlord, you deal damage instead of killing them.

the fellowship's armies

You have taken control of an army. Choose any Companion from the Innocents list that makes sense - you have three Bonds with them, and they gain the Army stat.

Your army can only be damaged by Dangerous or Area attacks, and they count as being everywhere in their location at the same time. You do not need to damage their stats to use their Moves - there are enough of them that they can always help you in whatever way they can.

to the gates of hell itself

Your Companions will never erase their last Bond with you.

you will not die this day

When an ally gets Taken Out and you dramatically rush to their side, you may share words with them, and nothing will interrupt you.

Afterwards, they choose one:

  • The Warlord will get them to safety, taking them out of the scene.

  • The Warlord can stand and fight, filled with Hope for the rest of the scene.

  • They get back up, heal 1 damage, and cannot hold Despair for the rest of the scene.

custom moves

potion's master

When you Fill Your Belly, you may instead spend 1

Food to brew a potion. It has 1 Use when brewed, and a potion's effects are never permanent. You can create a basic Healing Potion (1 Use, Healing), or something a bit more unusual. When brewing an unusual potion, describe what it looks like, tell us what it does, and then choose two:

  • It is a cream and must be applied; it has the Slow tag.

  • It gets the imbiber Drunk.

  • It is expensive - spend 1 Use of anything to finish it.

  • It has a weird side effect, which you'll discover when you drink it.

stitched together

When you sew up a dying or recently dead body

and breathe some magic into it, they return to life, whether they like it or not. You gain a Bond with them, and they are both living and dead.

turn you into a newt

When you wish to conjure a hex upon them, state

the full name of someone you can see and the reason for your curse. Hexes are narrative - tell us what they lose and what they gain, such as losing arms to gain wings, losing claws to gain suction cups, or losing speech to gain gills.

Your hexes always end at dawn's first light. Hexes never work on a Threat to the World. If you try to hex another player, they may attempt to Overcome your hex after you tell them what it is. Your hexes can never be directly lethal, such as taking away the ability to breathe.

Otherwise, your hexes always work every time.

witch's familiar

You have one Bond with a Familiar you have created.

Bonds you have with your Familiar cannot be broken, and you do not need to damage your Familiar to use the Move Bound In Service with them.

Familiar: A small animal-like creature you have created to serve you. It looks like it was plucked from your dreams; describe it. It can share its senses with you, speak your languages, and function in your place in diplomatic matters.
