Cyborg

Destiny Playbook

You have rebuilt yourself, and become stronger for it.

Requirements

To become the Cyborg, you must have taken technology to a new level. This is arbitrary and up for player interpretation, but generally, if you want to be the Cyborg, you have to do technology things first.

The Cyborg starts with a Cyborg Custom Move of their choice.
